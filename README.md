## About

Don't Litter is a 3D platformer and adventure game where the player explores 5 theme-based levels each with unique obstacles, enemies, and puzzles. The player controls a warrior type character who can walk/run, jump and attack enemies. Powerups and attack upgrades will be acquired as the player progresses on in the game. The player also has health, and enemy attacks and environmental hazards can hurt the player.

## Story

The game follows the journey of young Atreyu who is sent on a mission from his village to properly dispose of a flask that contains dark magic. However, instead of safely getting rid of the flask by going deep into the heart of the forest, Atreyu is hungry and decides to throw the flask off a cliff so that he can rush back home to eat. Unbeknownst to him, the flask lands right on the head of a sleeping golem, waking it up. The golem is filled with rage and begins a rampage, destroying the village and wreaking havoc before Atreyu can even make it back.

When Atreyu finally arrives back at the village, he goes to talk to the sage, who informs him of the golem's whereabouts. Now, instead of spending this time enjoying a nice meal, Atreyu sets out on a quest to track down the golem before it causes more destruction. 

Atreyu travels high and low, from forest to temple, and when he finally meets the golem face-to-face, it directs him to the Lava World, where the golem reveals its true Obsidian Form. The ever-more destructive nature of the obsidian golem and the extreme dangers of the Lava World leave Atreyu no choice but to resort to advanced warfare tactics to survive and defeat the golem once and for all.
